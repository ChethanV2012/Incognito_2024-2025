About Incognito

Welcome to Incognito

Incognito is the annual inter-collegiate IT Fest organised by The Department of Computer Science and Post Graduate Department of Computer Applications.

Incognito is more than just an event—it’s a movement towards hands-on learning and innovation. Our goal is to challenge participants with real-world tech problems, sparking creativity and a competitive edge. From coding challenges to groundbreaking projects, this event is a platform for young minds to showcase their skills, collaborate, and push the boundaries of technology. Join us in this exciting journey of discovery, growth, and innovation. Let’s make this year’s Incognito an experience to remember!
